# History
After having left home for several years she finds the door laying busted on the ground with the smell of blood heavy in the air. "Mom?! Dad?!" _her face goes pales as her skips a beat_ Growling and snarling can be heard from within the house as she, with her heart in her throat, quickly bolts into the house to find her parents being eaten alive by two, ugly blood soaked two-headed beasts. Having run into the shouting she had gotten the attention of the two ugly beasts as they slowly turned towards her, snarling and dripping with blood from their mouths, she picked up her feet and bolted into the local park as quickly as she could. With tears streaming down her face, she begins to scream bloody murder for help, but to no avail as the surroundings seemed eerily quiet. Even in her sorrow she had sensed something was wrong with the neighborhood but now wasn't the time to focus on that, as she would turn her gaze backwards to check if the two monsters were following her. Seeing one of the beasts following in hot pursuit of her, and the other stopping at the door way as it then quickly disappeared back away from sight. Stopping in the center of the park Field she braces herself, wiping away her tears as she puffs up her chest letting out an echoing scream. "Why did you have to kill them!? They were my family! My Mother, My Father!" _Her mind starts to race with images of burning this monstrosity to the ground. With flames erupting from her hands. With the head of this monster in her hands._ Then her once cold pale hands come to life with Blazing blue tinted flames. Feeling the flames spark to life in her hands she would lunge at the beast's faces and plunge them into its coarse brown fur covered face, attempting to burn its face to cinders. The beast, in a frenzied panic, slams its whole body into the girl, knocking her aside and digging its sharp blood soaked fangs into her shoulders. She screams in agonizing pain at the sensation of the two headed dogs bite. Almost as quickly as the intense pain hit her, it no longer felt as intense. With a burst of strength she grabs the snout of one of the two heads, and squeezes it with all her might. With a combination of desperate strength and heat from the searing flames, she manages to pry the top of the jaw off her, causing it to sear her hand into it. The left side head had clamped harder, shaking its head trying to tear the muscles in her shoulder out. Unfazed by it she repeats her actions onto the left head, causing the grip the beast had on her to cease entirely. Pulling herself together for a moment, she takes a deep breath in, and then out. She begins to think to herself "I have slayed this dog? But can I do it to the other..." She pulls herself together and takes a deep breathe, and beings to collect her thoughts*
