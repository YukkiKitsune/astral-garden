# Backstory
A Paladin not by choice, but by necessity. He was brought up in a lowly village as a child with his family.  
Living on the outskirts of town in the wilderness with his parents and sister. He was a small boy still when, one night, he heard a commotion happening outside. Peaking through a window, he saw his father, pitchfork in hand, fending off a hound-like creature. Before seeing his father maimed in front of him. Out of instinct to protect his sister and mother, he grabs a small sword his father kept attempting to prevent the beast from entering his home, but he failed. With one swipe from the hound ripping through his face, leaving him bleeding on the floor. Through his blood-soaked eyes, he witnessed as the creature and his remaining family brawled. Mother and sister were both fighting for their lives, striking the beast as best they could. Mother is being shoved back against the wall with such force that it leaves her unable to walk. The sister continued to fight, only for the jaws of the beast to lock around her arm, ripping the flesh and muscle alike. Luckily or perhaps unluckily for him, a passing adventurer overheard the screams from down the road. Storming in with a weapon in hand, the adventurer managed to slay the now-injured beast. The beast was now defeated, but not before his family had suffered grievous wounds. They were then brought into the city of Blackforge, requiring vigorous care to treat their wounds. His mother was paralyzed from the waist down from the blow, his sister is now unable to move one of her arms, and him left with an abhorrent scar. This care came at a price, a price that his now crippled family could not pay. Being that he was the only able-bodied person left, he was forced to pay off their medical debts. He was seen as a proxy criminal due to his inability to pay his own debts. Valerius worked through many years of his life in intensive physical labor alongside murderers, thieves, and much worse. This environment made him, hearing the stories, made him grow to despise all in this world that is morally incorrect. Solidifying his passion for things that are unjust. His time in this required him to be quick-tongued, physically strong, and mentally powerful. He grew to become a brute of a man, standing at near 7 foot tall with a brass demeanor, holding the scars of his past on his face. As his debt was finally paid off, he sought out to further himself in the Academies of Luminastra in the pursuit of becoming what he truly desired, a Paladin. Ultimately, wanting to destroy all that he perceives to be evil in this world. His family pleaded with him not to go, stating that it was time to forgive and forget. But this time in this world had changed him, changed his heart. His new passion and love was no longer for things like family, but justice.
