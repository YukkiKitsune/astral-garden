# Backstory
A boy who was brought up in a home far from the reaches of others. Brought up by a lone father who was an outcast known for being very proficient in the ways of magic. As a child he had learned the ways of magic from his father, but he lacked a talent for it. Most of his confidence comes from that of his studies of the books his father's old study. Due to this he likes to act more knowledgeable than others when it comes to things even without knowing that those books are of times far gone. As the boy got older he grew more interested in the world outside his small house out in the middle of nowhere and wished to venture out to know what the people of this world were like. Which lead to his father bringing up the idea of going to academy. Upon learning of this the boys eyes sparkled with the idea of this and began his prep to go to academy. Upon the day of his departure his father gave him something to help give the boy comfort and protection for his journey's and life outside the only place he ever knew.
