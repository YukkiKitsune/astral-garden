# Description
Rachel, the original vampire. A progenitor and a powerful sorcerer in her own right. Driven by the desire to protect all she cares for in this world. Willing to go to any extent in the pursuit of ensuring those under her care maintain safety. Overall elusive and mysterious of a woman, she still has the desire to protect.

# History
Rachel was born in the early stages of this world, born in the 47th year. She was born a regular human before growing into being a powerful sorcerer capable of great things. She saw the dangers and potential of this world and knew her own power would not be enough. As she aged she could feel her powers beginning to diminish, she began to experiment by any means necessary to gain power. During this time of experimentation and failed attempts the primordial Lera noticed her strife. Lera felt moved from seeing Rachel's extreme desire to protect, then decided to bestow upon her the abilities of being a vampire. Using this power she would aim to protect her own civilization. She succeeded for ages until a new power arose, the Fey. In the year 4173 one of the first, most unstable of the Fey portals would arrive on top of her own civilization. She watched as the unstable fey energy began ripping through her once beloved city until there was nothing left. She now has a goal of vengeance against the Fey, knowing she does not have enough power in of herself to stop such a force she now pursues research and allies in her goal.
