# Backstory
From a young age Azshara was always one who delved head first into her studies, though her family never liked what trouble she would get into and often scorn her for it, they still encouraged her studies. They believed she would turn into a strong Magus someday like her family who all were strong magic users, like her father was a magus and her mother was a priest. Although her younger brother did not have a huge interest in magic, which was not all that uncommon for her family, you were more expected to become one. Growing past her childhood years where she was constantly surrounded by magic teachers who praised her for her natural abilities at magic though when she found texts regarding fel magic she would ask her teachers about it and she was warned about how dangerous it was and that it was a very bad magic. She would take to their warning but was curious about it, seeing as how it was so frowned upon. During this time she would hang out with her brother who was an outcast by the family, he was shunned and frowned upon for taking interest in the martial arts so she would often hangout with him and read. She would show him what she learned and do physical training with him during her down times from studying. She had gotten rather close with her brother until she was sent away by her family to go into the Magi university of suramar around her 87th birthday. Though she kept in contact with her brother as much as she could while studying at the university. During her time there she began to sneak awake into the late hours and study magic and accidentally created fel magic when she was experimenting with void magic to decide which magic she would stick with. She remembered reading up on what she had created in some books before but besides what the adults told her about the burning legion she did not know much more. She began to research it in her own free time after feeling the thrill of what she had created with her own hands. Although curiosity was starting to get the better of her she didn't care much, she would drink an invisibility potion and sneak past the teachers to the archives where all wonders of knowledge was stored, there she would steal some of the books and bring them back with her to the middle of the forest in a clearing she had found to study and practice on her own. Barely being able to read a single word of these books she would find a way and begin to translate them with books containing the ancient language cypher. Some scholars had recorded a decent cipher to translate any books or texts that they found in the future for study and record keeping. She would after many hours of scanning through texts and trying and failing she would produce a green flame at the tip of her finger instead of an accidental explosion. 

  

Through all of this she would still attend class regularly being able to make her way though as an average student. She would proceed to graduate at the bottom of her class during this time though she would be sending word to her brother excited about how much progress she was making though she went home after graduating to being banished from home for being a failure like her brother and was outcasted because of how stuck up her parents were. She never saw this side of them because of how engrossed in her studies she was, but she failed school and than got basically kicked out of her own home. She never saw her brother upon returning ethier so she believes she was sent away but she would soon after coming home close to her 100th birthday would leave and end up finding underground suramar covens dedicated to warlocks. 

  
  
  
  
  

The years passed and Azshara had spent that time learning amongst new teachers and comrades while she had missed her brother she never knew what had happened to him. She spent this time making various friends of many races, Among them being various scorned faces hiding away from the light like worgen’s and draenei. Though as time had passed they had received news of the Horde and Alliance beating Elisande. Shortly after was close to her 109th birthday and she would only be spending more time with them until she was deemed ready to wonder out into the world using her own powers for whatever she deemed, but before that some of the friends she made had helped her one last time to sneak back to her household and confirm that her family was still their, though they knew what was going to happen they just made sure the cost would be clear for her to leave after she finished her business with her family.  

  

Azshara, much more knowledgeable, stronger, and devious than before, With true friends and a new family she had finally seen her opportunity for exact revenge. She decided to join her family at the dinner table for the first time in many years. Something that completely threw her family off guard. Does she want to rebuild a connection with us? Did she become a great mage or get some great achievements? These are the types of questions that went through their heads as they prepared dinner for that night. Sadly for them, Azshara had a much more devious plan in store. The moment she opened the door, she'd tell her parents that she had a very pleasant surprise to share with them. Something wonderful that her favorite professor had bestowed upon her. One can only think that what her family had in mind was her talent in arcana. Waiting with anticipation, she finally stands before them. Arms out wide as she hymns fel textures. Her father, seeing such things before, tries to stop her but too little too late as energy surges through her, green flames emit from her hands and a wave of green flames burst through the house burning it down in an instant. There was only a brief silence and screaming as her parents were burning in the flames, though they never saw something like this coming they half expected something of this matter to be done after all the years of misery they put their children through.  
  

There were only a few moments of shock before Azshara as she had unleashed her decades of pent up wrath upon them. As her entire family immolated in the fel, The smell of sulfur and smoke would fill the air that night as she slipped away with her new family helping her escape. Though none would know of what transpired except for a warlock burning this house and family alive none would ever come to know it was her, as even the underground warlock coven that had raised her became fairly close to her and wouldn't set out to expose their gifted new family member and friend to unwanted attention. Though this town would never see such events again, except for the rare wild warlock or experiment getting out of hand, they would not know of what truly happened today.  
  
Years had passed after that, it was now close to her 125th birthday where in those years she spent gaining knowledge in the wild on her own and drinking with new friends she would make and seeking out more power. She would find news of some events going down in an arctic town dunmowrogh and finding her way their in time around her 127th year were she met some interesting folks and decided on a whim to hang around them to see where this would take them, or if need be finding a new path to set forth on. She was not afraid to make more friends but was wary to trust anyone new.
