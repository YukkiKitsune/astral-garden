# Summary
A once powerful and well known figure
The reaper. 
A strange and powerful being who is able to connect places together that otherwise could not, can also connect souls to such places. 

The city that appears in the desert in a mirage will be the forgotten kings story. He did his absolute best to always try to provide for his people, to go and make a fair and livable place, but instead after hunting down his enemies, and coming home to a loving town. They all died, slaughtered by the very ones he swore to protect. His people were slaughtered by the allies he made because they were scared of him, they thought of him as someone who needed to die, to afraid to kill him they would rather have his people die for him, and then team up with the very enemies he fought against to protect them from. 

Aurekthal was an immortal king whose reign over a small desert town was defined by defiance, sovereignty, and an unwavering belief in self-rule. His rule was not born from desire but from a challenge he issued and accepted—one that lasted for generations.
