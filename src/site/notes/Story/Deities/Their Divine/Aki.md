# Description
Outer god of the Fey Wilds. Aki was the original overseer god of the Fey Wilds and came from the old connecting universe. When the new universe was created, the primordials accidentally tapped into an already existing Fey Wilds while constructing the current universe. Aurekthal had severed the old Fey Wilds shortly after their creation and allowed some old lesser gods, such as the Fey Courts, into the Fey Wilds. Aurekthal banned Aki from leaving the Fey Wilds; if he wished to stay, he was granted free will over the Fey Wilds and was allowed to continue to exist. However, if he was ever seen outside the Fey Wilds—aside from an avatar—he would be destroyed on the spot.
