# Backstory
Gale was attacked by the general leader of his hometown and out of self-defense he murdered the leader and left before anyone can see, but he left behind evidence leading to him, after the residents of his hometown learned of the murder they attempted to kill him by burning down his home while he slept, but the smell of the smoke woke him early enough to escape the burning building he then fled his hometown and now rummage the streets stealing from and robbing passerby's in order to survive these lands. The sight of smoke will forever remind him of the events that forced him out scaring him deathly.
