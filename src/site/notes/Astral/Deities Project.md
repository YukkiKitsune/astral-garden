Name: Alice

Age: 18+ (Is ageless)

Race: Unknown

Role: Town priestess

  

Appearance:  pure white complexion and long white hair. Wears a priestess kimono. Has red eyes and slime body and features. Is shorter than her friends only around 5'0

Personality: kind and warm to everyone. Is very mature for her age and loves everyone

Bio: adopted child of seol and nunbola lives in shrines. She usually makes her own shrine to travel between. She loves her friends and usually has a cute little shrine decoration and several tattoos so she can freely travel with her companions. Has many companions of various species and races.

Gender: Genderless but has feminine appearance that she dawns more often.
