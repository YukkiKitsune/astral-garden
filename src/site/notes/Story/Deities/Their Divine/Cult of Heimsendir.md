This god is secretly disguise itself as the goddess of death due to her being a very prominent figure in the world. 
This god is going to one day make sure that the entire world is brought into the dark ages of the gods, and staying hidden by blending in and trying to slowly gain more followers. 
Unlike some worlds where they pray to the gods and that god knows, they have no real way you are trying to pray to them as there is no way to communicate with them. 
Instead cults pop up all the time of people who have either "talked" to the gods or has been a prophet of one. Some claims to have seen a god descend but in reality the gods never descend as they are, often times they where around during the first epoch to try and build and shape the world as is, but after that they all vanished. It feels as if something either forced them to vanish or they vanished due to having done their job and not wanting to intervene with mortal affairs. 
This world to all the gods is like a project made by their higherups to complete. Once they completed it they let it sit to monitor it and hope that the world itself can fix it to be a natural world. 
But lurking among the first created was a god that simply was born with to much ego, kept having conflicts with the other gods and sometimes tried to berate the primordials for not doing a proper job and actually doing well. They soon forgot about the deity when one day they vanished after having created humans. 


in the middle of the ocean there is a floating island that connects to the realm of the divine where all the gods both dead and alive rest. The floating tavern that is used lies there but it is also outside the world itself over looking it.
