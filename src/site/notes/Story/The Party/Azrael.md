# Backstory
As a child I lived with just my father. My mother died during childbirth. My father was an inventor and made little gadgets to sell as a street merchant. So as a child I knew I wanted to make a difference and become an inventor as well, but as soon as I turned 20 I joined the military. I was a combat engineer crafting weaponry for our military forces. After 8 years of service, I just got out of the military, I lost my right arm from an explosion during an invasion. Which to continue my service I crafted my own prosthetic arm with my crafting tools implemented inside of it I had seen gruesome combat and had caused many casualties with the weaponry I made and manufactured. Every so often I have ptsd breakdowns due to the things I’ve done and caused but I know it was all for the greater good. They only trigger after intense combat situations.
