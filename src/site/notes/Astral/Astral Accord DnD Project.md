# [[Story/Locations/Athbhreith\|Athbhreith]] (The World)
## Moons
2 Moons
## Continents
- Virelia - Loads of forests, mountains and vast plains (think like Americas)
- Borealis - Primarily cold continent that shares land with the demon continent. Has some non cold biomes like Mountains and frozen deserts 
- Karnathis - The Demon Continent, a place filled with Jungles, Valleys, Lakes and Volcanos
- Frostholm - North most Continent, Has Fjords, Ice fields, frozen lakes, Vast frozen over canyons
- Cindermoor - Often mistakes as an island but its just mostly hidden behind a veil, the continent is super hot and has lots of deserts and humid forests
- Mythralis - Has no civilization but is full of legendary monsters and beasts, Has very active rainforests, Swamps, and Valleys
- Vastara - A continent with little civilization but holds tons of Badlands, and Ravines. Little trees grow in this land
- Thalorim - A vast land with deep seas and rugged mountains, and few volcanoes
- Solara - A central continent that holds trade and many different races, it is the center most land mass to the world. Is a wonderland of different biomes.

#### Time
- Moon cycle length: 21 days
- Number of cycles per year: 13
- Total days in the year: 273 days

- 1 week = 6 days (your week length)
- 3.5 weeks = 3.5 × 6 days = 21 days (Month)
- 30 hour days, 12 hours of light, 18 hours of dark.
