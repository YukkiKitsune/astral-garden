# Overview
A celebrated apothecary and crude scientist. A master of the crafts, betrayed by the kingdom she cherished, she dedicated herself to curing those it had abandoned. With her mastery of the arcane arts, she fought to rescue those subjected to the kingdom's cruel experiments.
# Pre-Ascension
An alchemist from an old, long-forgotten world, She is usually the core of every plan that goes on inside the new world, yet he is very quiet. She used to be an alchemist for a war-hungry kingdom that wanted to control the world through its own power, but they ended up causing the world to fall.

The world was wrought with death from diseases. The kingdom had neglected the majority of the people under its control. It would constantly use its citizens for experiments to try and advance its own power. The countless bodies that racked up after the experiments were thrown back into the slums where they were taken from. The ones that lived stayed in the castle, becoming repeated candidates for tests, or being drafted into the military to fight in wars for more land. The usable lands had started to diminish after all the bodies started piling up, and the miasma grew worse and worse, to the point people had to use hazard suits to enter or exit these districts. Min was one of the top alchemists for this empire. He was the one to help them come up with new ways to enhance its people, using whoever had been given to him for the tests. He was able to slowly create a way for the upper class to avoid aging. He had been awarded a team of researchers to enhance the army to look for more land and fight for it; he was able to use this power to conquer more of this world until there was nothing left. After a certain point, the world was no longer usable for most people; the death toll was so high that it became impossible to gather resources in most areas. The only clean areas left were used by the ultra-wealthy to live out their lives in peace. They would use all the resources gathered to support their gluttonous lifestyle.
